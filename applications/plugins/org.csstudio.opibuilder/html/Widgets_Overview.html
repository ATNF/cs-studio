Widgets Overview

Widget is the brick to build your OPI.All the currently available widgets are listed in the Widget Palette. There could be more widgets to be added in future. Benefits from the plug-in mechanism of Eclipse, you may also add your own widgets by implement a new widgets plug-in.

There are four types of widgets:

  • Graphics: The graphics widgets can help you decorate your OPI.
  • Monitors: The widgets that can graphically display the value of PV in various ways.
  • Controls: The widgets by which you can make control operations directly.
  • Others: The other widgets which is not in the three major categories.
